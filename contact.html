Frequently Asked Questions

Can I replicate your experiment?

Absolutely! We've documented our methodology in detail on the Research page. We encourage others to replicate our findings. It's one of the hallmarks of good science. If you do recreate the experiment, we'd love to hear about your results!

What if I get different results?

Different results could be due to variations in equipment, materials, environmental factors, or measurement techniques. This is a valuable scientific insight! We recommend documenting all variables carefully and comparing your procedure with ours. Such variations help us understand the bounds and limitations of the findings.

Can I use this project for my own research assignment?

Our work is shared for educational purposes. If you use our project as a reference or build upon our findings, please credit us appropriately and follow your school's academic integrity policies. Science progresses through building on previous work!

Do you offer workshops or demonstrations?

We're passionate about science education! While schedules vary, we may be available for school presentations or science fair demonstrations. Contact us to discuss possibilities.

How long did this project take?

From initial concept to final website publication, the project spanned 6 weeks. This included research, planning, equipment setup, data collection across multiple trials, analysis, and documentation. We believe quality scientific work takes time!
